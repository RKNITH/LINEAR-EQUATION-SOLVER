Linear Equation Solver

A linear equation is a mathematical equation that represents a straight line when graphed on a Cartesian coordinate system. It has the general form:

ax + by + cz = d

Where:

  • x, y, and z are variables representing coordinates in space,
  • a, b, and c are the coefficients of the variables, and
  • d is the constant term.

Linear equations are fundamental in mathematics and have many applications in various fields such as physics, engineering, economics, and computer science.
